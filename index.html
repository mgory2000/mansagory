Biography of Mansa Gory

I am a professional musical artist with over 17 years of experience as a Band Director and a Master of Music Education. I also am immensely steeped in computer science, but still a hobbyist. In my current position as Band Director for the Mount Vernon Performing and Visual Arts Magnet, I conduct the jazz band, concert band, modern band, and percussion ensemble. I also founded and manage a community youth enrichment musical organization, The I.C.E. Cold Experience, in with children from diverse backgrounds assemble to learn repertoire and compete against collegial groups. I am highly organized, detail oriented, and efficient as is proven by my ability to manage the number of requirements involved in running multiple successful school music groups. I have solid communication skills and make a concerted effort to reach out to band parents and to keep students volunteers and parents consistently informed of important matters. Most importantly I have a solid understanding of musical concepts and a flair for production My personal philosophy is; “The Arts bring communities together for higher achievement.”
